Courses

Graduate and undergraduate courses at the O’Donnell Institute draw on our  scholars’ broad-ranging areas of specialization, and focus on artworks and other research materials held in Dallas-Fort Worth collections.

Our recent and upcoming courses include

  • Inca: Conquests of the Andes (Dr. Kimberly Jones, Fall 2015),
  • Form as Meaning (Dr. Max Schich, Fall 2015),
  • Delacroix/Gauguin (Dr. Rick Brettell and Dr. Beth Wright, Spring 2016),
  • Arts of the Islamic World  (Dr. Sabiha Al Khemir, Spring 2016),
  • The Art of Examination at UT Southwestern Medical School (Prof. Bonnie Pitman, Spring 2016),
  • The Portrait (Dr. Mark Rosen, Fall 2016), and
  • The Painter at Work (Dr. Sarah K. Kozlowski).
