Scholars Affiliated with the O’Donnell Institute

Scholars affiliated with the O’Donnell Institute form an intellectual community dedicated to collaboration and exchange.

The O’Donnell Institute of Art History draws together scholars from The University of Texas at Dallas, The University of Texas at Arlington, and the Dallas Museum of Art.

Together, O’Donnell Institute scholars form an intellectual community dedicated to collaboration and exchange. For the 2015-2016 academic year the O’Donnell Institute also welcomes four Visiting Research Fellows and six Edith O’Donnell Graduate Fellows.
