James Magee and The Hill

In 2010 Rick Brettell, Founding Director of the O’Donnell Institute, Jed Morse, Chief Curator at the Nasher Sculpture Center, and photographer Tom Jenkins collaborated to produce a volume that documents James Magee’s site-based project, The Hill, in far west Texas.
